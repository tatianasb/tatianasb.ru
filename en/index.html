I like pretty things. I enjoy looking at something beautiful even if it's raw, like a sketch or a draft. It's beautiful because it's the start of something new. I like being part of it, creating and bringing an ideia (your idea!) to life.

Born in Russia, living most of my life in Brazil, can never get enough of traveling. I'm passionate about: design, fashion, makeup, food, animals and sports (not necessarily in that order).

I hold a BA in Publicity and Advertisement and once upon a time took a course on graphic design. For the past 10 years, I've been gaining experience by working and by learning from amazing people that come into my life.
